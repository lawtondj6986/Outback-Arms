New to Massachusetts Firearms Law?

Buying a gun in Massachusetts — the right way, in four steps.

MA has its own rules, and we walk every customer through them. No surprises at the counter.

1
Get your LTC or FIDApply through your local police department. We host Mass-approved safety courses every Saturday — you'll get the certificate you need to apply.
2
Pick your firearmEverything on our floor is Massachusetts-compliant. Handguns come from the state's approved roster, so what you see is what you can legally buy.
3
Background check & paperworkWe run the federal check and file the state's electronic record right at the counter. Bring your license — we handle the rest, usually in minutes.
4
Take it homeMost approved sales walk out the same day with a trigger lock included, as MA safe-storage law requires. Out-of-state buyer? We ship to your local FFL.
ℹ️Firearms laws change. This is a plain-English overview, not legal advice — our staff verifies current Massachusetts requirements on every sale, and we'll happily answer licensing questions by phone at 781-585-6615.

Answers Before You Ask

Frequently asked questions.

How long does a background check take in Massachusetts?
Usually just a few minutes. Massachusetts uses an electronic system at our counter, so approved buyers with a valid LTC or FID almost always walk out the same day — trigger lock included, as state law requires.
Do you do FFL transfers, and what's the fee?
Yes — bought a gun online or from an out-of-state seller? Have them ship it to Outback Arms LLC, 376 Main Street, Plympton, MA 02367. We charge a flat per-firearm transfer fee that covers the background check and paperwork. We'll text you the moment it arrives.
How do I get my Massachusetts LTC or FID card?
First you need a state-approved firearms safety certificate, then you apply through your local police department. We run Mass-approved safety classes every Saturday that give you exactly that certificate. Reserve a seat →
Is everything on your site actually MA-compliant?
Yes. Every firearm on our floor is legal to sell in Massachusetts, and handguns come from the state's approved roster. What you see listed is what you can legally buy here — no bait-and-switch at the counter.
How does a trade-in work?
Bring your firearm in (or send photos) and we'll make an honest offer — cash, store credit at a higher value, or consignment. We log every transaction legally through our FFL. Try the quick estimator for a ballpark first.
Can you ship a gun to me?
Firearms ship dealer-to-dealer only. If you're out of the area, we ship to a licensed FFL near you and you complete the transfer and background check there. Ammo and accessories can ship where legal.
Can I put a gun on hold?
Absolutely. Use the "Hold This Gun" button on any product or the intake form. We'll hold it up to 48 hours while we confirm details — no payment required to start.
Do I need to be 21? What about long guns?
You must be 21+ for handguns and ammunition, and hold the correct MA license. Rifles and shotguns may be available to 18+ FID holders. Our staff will confirm exactly what your license allows — just ask.
